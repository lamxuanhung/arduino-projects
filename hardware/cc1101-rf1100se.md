CC1101 - RF1100se 
=================

Purchased from [Deal Extreme] [0].

SPI - Arduino UNO R3
--------------------

| SPI  | UNO R3 | RF1100se |
|------|--------|----------|
| VCC  | 5v     | 1 (vcc)  |
| GND  | GND    | 9 (gnd)  |
| MOSI | 11     | 3        |
| MISO | 12     | 5        |
| SS   | 10     | 7        | 
| SCK  | 13     | 4        |
| GDO0 |  2     | 8        |
